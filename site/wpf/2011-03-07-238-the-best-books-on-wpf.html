#238 – The Best Books on WPF

2011-03-07 | Original URL | Wayback 20110702231944

March 7, 2011 by Sean Leave a Comment

Here are some of my favorites books on WPF, or related to developing software using WPF:

  • Pro WPF in C# 2010: Windows Presentation in .NET - Matthew MacDonald
    • Excellent book, maybe the best book out there on WPF; very complete
  • WPF 4 Unleaded – Adam Nathan
    • Another excellent resource; full color; great explanations of topics like 3D graphics
  • WPF Control Development Unleashed: Building Advanced User Experiences – Pavan Podila & Kevin Hoffman
    • Nice book that goes into topics that some of the other books don’t cover; includes good patterns for developing controls
